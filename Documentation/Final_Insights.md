# Final Business Insights

## Customer Retention

- Built a cohort-based retention analysis to understand repeat customer behavior.
- Identified customers with only one purchase as potential churn customers.

## Customer Behavior

- Calculated repurchase gaps using the LAG() window function.
- Tracked future purchases using the LEAD() window function.

## Customer Segmentation

- Ranked customers based on total revenue using RANK() and DENSE_RANK().
- Identified high-value customers contributing the highest revenue.

## Marketing Attribution

- Implemented First-Touch Attribution using ROW_NUMBER().
- Implemented Last-Touch Attribution to evaluate the final marketing channel before purchase.
- Compared revenue generated by different acquisition channels.

## Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing budget on top-performing channels.
- Launch customer retention campaigns for one-time buyers.
- Introduce loyalty programs for high-value customers.
- Target inactive customers with personalized email campaigns.
